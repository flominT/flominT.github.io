About me

I am currently a PhD student at the IRSN and IFSTTAR labs. I work under the supervision of Dr. Fabian Bonilla. My research interests include numerical simulations of seismic wave propagation in complex media, ambient noise interferometry, site effect analysis and machine learning applied to seismology. Prior to joining IRSN, I completed my graduate studies majoring in Geophysics at the University of Grenoble Alpes with focus on signal processing, inverse methods and computational seismology. My graduate research was conducted under the supervision of Pr. Michel Campillo.
I am also a huge fan of python, web development, football (Messi is my favorite player), basketball (Lebron is my favorite as well), photography, and chess.
